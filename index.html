About

Thank you for visiting my page!

I am a C# .NET web developer, passionate about technology, driven, disciplined and a great team-player. My first exposure to technology was at age 7 when my parents started an "Internet Cafe" on the top floor of our home in Lima, Peru. My task was to open doors and guide our clients to the computers.

Ever since, I have been fascinated by the way technology brings people together and improves our lives. I was lucky enough to pursue my undergraduate degree at Santa Clara University, in the heart of the Silicon Valley, where I further developed my interest in technology. Today, I live in Austin, Texas and I am looking forward to exploring job opportunities, joining a developer team and becoming part of Austin's great collaborative and booming technology environment.

If I am not coding I enjoy playing table tennis, cooking Peruvian-creole food, eating authentic Japanese Ramen and playing video games.
